This document defines the benefit programs available to employees along with compliance requirements and eligibility rules.

1. Health Benefits

Offered plans:
- PPO
- HMO
- High Deductible Health Plans (HDHP)
- Dental & Vision
- Mental health support programs
All plans comply with ACA coverage requirements.

2. Retirement Plans

Supported retirement options:
- 401(k) (traditional & Roth)
- Employer matching
- Vesting schedules
- Automatic enrollment rules

3. Life Events & Eligibility

Employees may modify benefits during:
- Open enrollment
- Qualifying life events (marriage, birth, adoption)
- Loss of other coverage
Changes must be submitted within 30 days of event.

4. COBRA & Termination Benefits

For U.S. employees, the company provides COBRA continuation coverage. Notices are automatically generated through HR+ and emailed to departing employees.

5. Compliance Programs

HR+ includes support for:
- ERISA reporting
- ACA 1095-C forms
- HIPAA privacy compliance
- Non-discrimination testing (NDT)
- Dependent eligibility audits
