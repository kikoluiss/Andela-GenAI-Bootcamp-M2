Payroll & Compensation Management Manual – Systems, Rules & Processing

This manual describes payroll operations, compensation structures, deduction rules, and compliance standards applicable organization-wide.

1. Pay Schedules

Supported payroll frequencies:
- Weekly
- Bi-weekly
- Semi-monthly
- Monthly
Pay groups support different schedules for salaried, hourly, and contractor employees.

2. Earnings & Pay Types

HR+ supports multiple earning codes:
- Regular earnings
- Overtime (1.5×)
- Double-time (2×)
- Holiday pay
- On-call pay
- Shift differential
- Commissions
- Bonuses (taxable and non-taxable)
- Retroactive adjustments

3. Overtime Calculation Engine

The payroll engine applies federal and regional laws. Configurable options:
- Fixed workweek (default: Sun–Sat)
- Daily overtime (California compliant)
- Weighted average overtime for variable rates
- Alternative workweek schedules
All calculations use ISO 20022-compliant rounding rules.

4. Deductions & Taxes
Supported deductions:
- Pre-tax (401k, HSA, FSA)
- Post-tax (garnishments, union dues, charity)
- Employer contributions
- Benefit premiums

The system calculates:
- Federal income tax (FIT)
- Social Security & Medicare (FICA)
- State income tax
- Local taxes (municipal, county)
HR+ integrates with government e-Filing services for automatic submission.

5. Direct Deposit & Payments

Employees may add up to 3 bank accounts. ACH transfers comply with NACHA standards. For international employees, SEPA and SWIFT transfers are supported.

6. Payroll Processing Workflow
- Import/validate timecards
- Validate PTO usage
- Review earnings and deductions
- Approve batch
- Generate pay stubs
- Trigger ACH processing
- File taxes electronically
- Update employee YTD records

7. Audits & Reporting

Payroll admins may run:
- YTD compensation reports
- Tax liability summaries
- Labor distribution reports
- Overtime audit logs
- Garnishment compliance reports
All exports support CSV, XLSX, and JSON formats.
